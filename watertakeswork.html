Water Takes Work

Campaign Awareness Animation

Roles: Design & Illustration Support, Animation

This motion graphic spot leverages data and clear visuals to explain the importance of our water systems to audiences both online & on broadcast television.

'Water Takes Work' is an awareness campagin funded by the State of Vermont.

It's easy to overlook the wonders of modern water infastructure. From hot showers at the turn of a knob to clean drinking water, we shouldn't take the marvel of our modern water systems and their workers for granted.

In collaboration with Place Creative, I helped to direct, and lead animation on this 30 second spot funded by the state of Vermont.
