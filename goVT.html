Better Streets

Campaign Awareness Animations

Roles: Art Direction, Animation, Sound Design

This animated series utilizes diverse cast of characters to underline how better streets can empower easier & safer mobility for everyone.

Local Motion and the VT Agency of Transportation came together to communicate the importance of accessible streets.

Vermont's transportation landscape continues to evolve to fit the needs of everyone. At the heart of this transition is the Vermont Agency of Transportation, as well as Local Motion, a statewide advocate for active transportation.

When Place Creative reached out to communicate this message, I focused my efforts on telling this story by creating colorful illustrations that allow for dynamic characters that walk, bike and roll their way through their environment. The end result was a dynamic, high-visibility video that helped to underline the message that strong communites are those that are safe for all travelers.
