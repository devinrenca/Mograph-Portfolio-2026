Putting beautiful things into motion

Driven by thoughtful, applicable design, Devin Renca has built a career as an adaptable visual designer. He specializes in all things motion-related; be it animation, motion graphics, or web interactions.

A New England Resident, Devin brings years of creative experience to the table, both as a member of a studio team and as a freelance creative. A graduate of Champlain College in Burlington Vermont, he earned his Bachelor of Fine Arts in Graphic Design.

Driven by a strong D.I.Y. mentality, Devin finds creative fufillment in different mediums. He enjoys creating through photography, woodworking and drawing. When not designing, he relaxes by jamming out to punk rock, goofing around with his pup Josie, or kicking around a soccer ball. Thanks for stopping by!
