The Long Island Sound Partnership

Mission Animation, Motion Identity

Roles: Art Direction, Animation, Sound Design

Rich with locations specific to the region, this animated spot communicates the mission of the Long Island Sound Program, as it heads into its 40th year anniversary.

The Long Island Sound Partnership is a collaborative effort between the EPA & the states of New York and Connecticut. The Program focuses on improving water quality in the Long Island Sound, as well as preserving the ecosystems that call that region home.

I had the pleasure of collaborating with the Program in celebration of their 40th anniversary. Working closely with the L.I.S.P. team, I led the project's art direction, illustrated and animated the final production. Tying everything together, I also created the motion for their new logo design.
