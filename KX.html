Yara: Knowledge Exchange

Event Motion Identity & Bumpers

Roles: Video Editing, Motion Graphics, Sound Design

Created for Yara International's 'Knowledge Exchange' summit, these event bumpers combine dynamic typography with striking video backgrounds.

Based in Oslo, Norway, Yara International is an global leader in agriculture, crop nutrition and cultivation. The Knowledge Exchange (KX) operates as the company's annual summit event.

Over the course of two iterations of the Knowledge Exchange event, I collaborated with the team over at Solidarity of Unbridled Labour to lead the development of motion graphics. The result is a package that combines thoughtful typographic animation, grounded videographic footage and subtle sound design.

During the event, the bumpers played on ceiling high, ultra-wide screens.
